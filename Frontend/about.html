About Ngultrum Classification

Welcome to the Ngultrum Recognition application, designed to simplify and streamline your experience with Bhutanese currency. Our app uses cutting-edge image recognition technology to instantly identify and classify Bhutanese Ngultrum (BTN) denominations in real-time. Whether you're a local or visitor, understanding the different denominations of the Ngultrum can sometimes be challenging. This app provides an easy-to-use solution, enabling you to instantly recognize the value of any Ngultrum note using your device's camera.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Currency Recognition: No need for manual input or complex calculations—simply use your camera to capture the image of the Ngultrum, and the app will automatically classify it for you.
  • Accuracy: Our deep learning model ensures high accuracy in identifying and classifying notes, even in varied lighting and conditions.

This application is designed to support various sectors by automating currency classification, making it easier to handle transactions, enhance financial applications, and more.
