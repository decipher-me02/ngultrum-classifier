Detect Your Ngultrums!

NuLens is a cutting-edge web application designed to simplify and automate the identification and classification of Bhutanese Ngultrum (BTN) currency denominations in real time using deep learning techniques.

How It Works

Simply allow access to your device's camera, and point it at a Bhutanese Ngultrum note. The system will automatically capture the image and classify the denomination in real-time.

About Nu Lens

Welcome to the Ngultrum Recognition application, designed to simplify and streamline your experience with Bhutanese currency. Our app uses cutting-edge image recognition technology to instantly identify and classify Bhutanese Ngultrum (BTN) denominations in real-time.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Currency Recognition: No need for manual input or complex calculations—simply use your camera to capture the image of the Ngultrum, and the app will automatically classify it for you.
  • Accuracy: Our deep learning model ensures high accuracy in identifying and classifying notes, even in varied lighting and conditions.

This application is designed to support various sectors by automating currency classification, making it easier to handle transactions, enhance financial applications, and more.

Attention!

We use the image captured from your device to store in our database for future implementations.
